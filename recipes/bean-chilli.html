Chipotle Bean Chilli

Description

A very simple but delicious chipotle bean chilli recipe with avocado and soured cream.

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice (75g p/serving)
  • Avocado (1/2 p/serving)
  • Kidney beans (100g p/serving)
  • Chilli powder (1tsp p/serving)
  • Ground cumin (1/2tsp p/serving)
  • Paprika (1/2tsp p/serving)
  • Chipotle paste (10g p/person)
  • Vegetable stock paste (5g p/serving)
  • Chopped tomatoes (100g p/serving)
  • Finely chopped onion (1/4 p/serving)
  • Baby spinach (20g p/serving)
  • soured cream (1tbsp p/serving)

Steps

  1. Boil enough water to cover your rice in a pan. Add your rice and cook for 10-12 mins.
  2. Once cooked, drain the rice and put it back in the pan with a lid over it until you are ready to add it to the frying pan later.
  3. Halve the avocado and remove the stone. Use a tablespoon to scoop out the flesh and cut it in to thick slices. Set aside.
  4. Add a little oil to a pan (medium heat) and fry the onions off until translucent, 7-8mins.
  5. Add in your spices and cook for another minute.
  6. Stir in the veg stock paste, finely chopped tomatoes, chipotle paste and kidney beans (add less chipotle if you prefer things milder). Add a pinch of sugar if you like.
  7. Wait until it starts to boil and then reduce the heat and simmer for 5-6mins..
  8. Stir the spinach through the chilli. Allow to cook for another 2-3 mins.
  9. Serve in to a bowl/bowls on top of your cooked rice and top with avocado and soured cream.
  10. Enjoy!
