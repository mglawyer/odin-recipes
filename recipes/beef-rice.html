Fragrant Beef Fried Rice

Description

A very simple but delicious fragrant beef fried rice recipe with pickled carrot and peanuts.

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice (75g p/serving)
  • Carrot (0.5 p/serving)
  • Rice vinegar (7.7ml p/serving)
  • Beef mince (120g p/serving)
  • Ginger, garlic & lemongrass puree (7.5g p/serving)
  • Peas (60g p/serving)
  • Ketjap manis (15g p/serving)
  • Sambal paste (10g p/serving)
  • Soy Sauce (15ml p/serving)
  • Salted peanuts (10g p/serving)
  • Sugar (1/2tsp p/serving)

Steps

  1. Boil some water. Add your rice and cook for 10-12 mins.
  2. Once cooked, drain the rice and put it back in the pan with a lid over it until you are ready to add it to the frying pan later.
  3. Trim the carrot and peel it in to thick strips.
  4. Add the carrot strips to a bowl with your rice vinegar and sugar and give it a toss. Set aside.
  5. Heat (medium-high)a frying pan and add your beef mince. Cook for 5-6 mins, until browned. Season with S&P.
  6. Add your paste and fry for another minute. Turn the heat down a little.
  7. Add the cooked rice and all other ingredients except the carrots and peanuts to the cooked mince.
  8. Add water (50ml p/serving) and stir throughly. Allow to cook for another 2-3 mins.
  9. Serve in to a bowl/bowls and top with crushed peanuts and pickled carrot.
  10. Enjoy!
