Fragrant Beef Fried Rice

Description

A very simple but delicious fragrant beef fried rice recipe with pickled carrot and peanuts.

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Carrot
  • Rice vinegar
  • Beef mince
  • Ginger, garlic & lemongrass puree
  • Peas
  • Ketjap manis
  • Sambal paste
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salted peanuts
