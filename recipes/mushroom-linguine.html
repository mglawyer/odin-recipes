Mushroom Linguine

Description

A very simple but delicious mushroom linguine recipe with bacon and asparagus.

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • Linguine pasta (90g p/serving)
  • Chopped mushrooms(40g p/serving)
  • Bacon lardons (30g p/serving)
  • Garlic clove (1 p/serving)
  • Asparagus tips (50g p/serving)
  • Creme fraiche (75g p/serving)
  • Chicken stock paste (5g p/serving)
  • Grated parmesan (15g p/serving)

Steps

  1. Boil enough water to cover your pasta in a pan. Add your pasta and cook for 12 mins (add chopped asparagus when there is 3 mins left).
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat a drizzle of olive oil in a frying pan on high heat. Once hot, add your mushrooms and bacon and cook for 4-5 mins.
  3. Meanwhile, peel and crush your garlic and trim and halve the asparagus tips.
  4. Add your garlic to the mushrooms and bacon and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. Add your asparagus to the cooking pasta if you haven't already.
  6. Reserve 100ml of the pasta water when you drain the pasta and drizzle the pasta with some olive oil
  7. Add the reserved water, creme fraiche, and chicken stock paste to the mushrooms and bacon.
  8. Add the pasta and asapargus too and mix together well.
  9. Add a little more water if the sauce is too thick.
  10. Enjoy!
